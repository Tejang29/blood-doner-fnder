About Blood Donation

Why Blood Donation Matters

Blood donation is a simple, safe way to help save lives. Every two seconds, someone in the United States needs blood. Your donation can help save up to three lives.

Regular donation helps maintain healthy iron levels

Our Services

Whole Blood Donation

Donate whole blood to help patients in emergency situations, surgeries, and cancer treatments.

Platelet Donation

Platelet donations help cancer patients and those with blood disorders.

Plasma Donation

Plasma donations help burn victims and patients with clotting disorders.

Mobile Blood Drives

We bring our donation services to your workplace, school, or community center.

Before You Donate

  • Be at least 17 years old
  • Weigh at least 55kg
  • Be in good health
  • Eat a healthy meal before donation
  • Bring a valid ID
